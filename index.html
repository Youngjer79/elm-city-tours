About ECT

Unique location to enjoy your life

At Elm City Tours this is your guide to what the wonderful city of New Haven have to offer.New Haven, CT is home to beautiful parks, excellent museums, unique attractions and a diverse choice of restaurants.The home of Yale University, the best pizza in the country, and creators of the hamburger. New Haven is an unique place with unique people of all backgrounds that make up the uniqueness of the city.

Things To Do

Things to do around the elm city such as take a visit to the Yale University Art Gallery, the Yale Peabody Museum of Natural History, and the New Haven Museum. Best things to do in New Haven, Connecticut with kids include the Connecticut Children's Museum and the Carousel at Lighthouse Point Park.
